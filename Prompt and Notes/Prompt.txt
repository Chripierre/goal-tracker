* Work beautifully on desktop, laptop, tablet, and mobile.

## Development Process



Do not rush into coding everything.



First:



1. Analyze the existing codebase.

2. Identify weaknesses.

3. Propose architecture improvements.

4. Design the complete application.

5. Explain major technical decisions.

6. Create an implementation roadmap.

7. Break the work into logical phases.

8. Then begin implementing one phase at a time.



At every stage, favor quality over speed. If you believe a different implementation would produce a significantly better long-term result, explain why and choose the superior approach rather than blindly following my requested implementation.
